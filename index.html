EXPERIENCE

IT Intern at City Government of Cabuyao, Laguna (March 2025 - June 2025)

During my internship with the City Government of Cabuyao, Laguna, I contributed to the design and maintenance of responsive websites using WordPress and Elementor, ensuring that online content was accessible, visually consistent, and aligned with the city’s public service goals. I also collaborated on digital presentations and materials that supported various local government initiatives, showcasing information in a clear and engaging way for both internal and public audiences. In addition, I provided basic troubleshooting and layout customization, helping improve workflow efficiency and ensuring smooth updates to the city’s online platforms.

As part of my academic capstone project, I led the development of a web-based mental health platform titled “ANDITO AKO.” The system enables students to book teleconsultations with counselors, offering both real-time chat functionality and an intuitive user interface designed to promote accessible and stigma-free mental health support. This project highlights my ability to integrate technical development with social impact, combining user experience design, backend logic, and secure communication systems to deliver a practical and meaningful digital solution.
